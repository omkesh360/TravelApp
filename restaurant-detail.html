About La Trattoria

Experience authentic Milanese dining in the heart of the city. La Trattoria Milano brings generations of family recipes to your table, featuring hand-made pasta, locally sourced ingredients, and an extensive wine list curated by our expert sommelier. Whether you are looking for a romantic dinner or a family gathering, our warm atmosphere welcomes you.

Our signature dishes include the traditional Osso Buco and the vibrant Saffron Risotto. We pride ourselves on offering a true taste of Northern Italy.
